Our Privacy Policy

Here's some information on the sort of personal information recieved and collected when you visit our site.

Logging

We collect data contained in log files.

The information in log files include your IP (internet protocol) address, the browser you use to visit the site, the time in which you visited th site, and the pages you visited.

Chances are, we'll never look at the log files - but we're obliged to tell you we do.

Cookies

Cookies are used on this site to store information concerning key functionality such as logging in and data caching. We do not use Google Analytics.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, this may adversely affect the functionality of the website.
